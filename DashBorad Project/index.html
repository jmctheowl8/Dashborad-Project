Dashborad Information

The USA (Ultrasound Student Assessment) is an examination of intrinsic skills that are essential for the sonography profession. The Austin Community College Sonography Programs will use its results to apply points towards the program application. The Sonography program focused on the physics and clinical categories of the assessment to award points on each applicant’s ranking worksheet. Here we looked at the correlation between the scores from these categories to the student’s actual performance during their first semester in the program. However, various other qualities are being evaluated under each of those categories, which can be seen in the report tab.
